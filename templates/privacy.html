Digital Constitution

v. 3.0.0 STABLE

Veracity operates on a zero-trust architecture. We believe that your search queries, API keys, and behavioral data belong strictly to you. The entire source code for this project is open-source, allowing you to audit our claims and verify exactly how data is routed and parsed. You can review the code repository at our official GitHub page.

We do not track, sell, or monitor individual user sessions. Your browser serves as the execution environment for storing credentials, maintaining strict segregation between your local machine and our servers.

analytics

Data Vectoring

Any queries you submit are passed directly to your selected API provider (e.g., Groq or Google) using your provided keys. We do not log or intercept the payloads on our backend.

  • LOCAL STORAGE ONLY
  • NO SERVER-SIDE TRACKING
  • NO PII COLLECTION
terminal

Purge Cycles

Because your data (history, keys, preferences) is stored entirely in your browser's localStorage, you retain absolute control. You may trigger a purge cycle at any time by clearing your browser data or clicking the 'Delete Sweep' icon in the dashboard.
