01

Acceptance of Terms

By interacting with the Veracity Truth Engine, you acknowledge and accept these operational protocols. Veracity serves as an analytical instrument. We do not generate the news; we aggregate, process, and evaluate it using third-party Language Processing Units (LPUs) and Large Language Models (LLMs).

"Veracity provides the lens; the truth is determined by the underlying data."

Because this project relies on live API endpoints, its uptime and accuracy are inherently tied to the stability of those upstream services (e.g., Groq, Google, NewsAPI). We are not liable for hallucinations or downtime originating from those providers.

02

User Conduct & Rate Limits

You are responsible for providing your own API keys for analysis. Consequently, you are bound by the Terms of Service and rate limits of those respective providers (Groq, Google Gemini, or Custom OpenRouter endpoints).
